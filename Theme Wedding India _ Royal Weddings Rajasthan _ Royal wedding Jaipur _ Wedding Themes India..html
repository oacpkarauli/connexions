About Us

“Dare to dream…. ‘Coz we make it real!!”

We are a professional event management firm with immense experience and expertise to overcome all the concerns you may have in planning and organizing any function or event in a manner you wish to execute it with the finest details in order.

We will help you make the right decisions to fit your budget. Your wishes and desires are the most important and we strive to make them a reality!

Connexions Event have always been known for the larger than life set ups and quality entertainment. We have been at your service with its full scale event management services, be it Corporate events viz; Seminars, Conferences, Exhibitions, Promotions, Weddings viz; royal weddings, destination weddings, international weddings, or Special events like private parties, Concerts, Birthdays etc.

We, as certified planners bear the responsibility to ensure every event from start to finish is designed to make you happy. We look into all the aspects that need to be taken care of from the most obvious to the finest details that meet your level of satisfaction.

We also provide a wide variety of creative ideas and impart current trends and styles to your setting. We offer you a wide variety of choice in the type of event you wish to host to ensure it befits your image and lifestyle. We believe in making every event a one-of a kind memory to all those present at the event.

Our team of professionals works on the philosophy of relationship building, which makes the core of our brand building mechanism. We understand the value of our prized customers and we try our best to put everything in place so the next time when sending invitations you proudly write on those beautiful cards ...please make your presence, it's Connexions Events.
